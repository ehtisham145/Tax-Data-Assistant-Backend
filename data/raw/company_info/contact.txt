Frequently Asked Questions
Is this platform FTA certified?
›
Yes — the platform is built against the UAE FTA Tax Accounting Software Certification Guidelines, capturing all required VAT FAF (21 fields) and Excise FAF (32 fields) data elements.
Which invoice types are supported?
›
Tax Invoice (UBL 380), Credit Note (UBL 381), Commercial Invoice (UBL 480), and Continuous Supply invoices are all supported.
How do I connect to the FTA network?
›
The platform uses the 5-corner model. In production, you connect through a UAE-accredited ASP (Accredited Service Provider) who routes invoices to the FTA.
Can I have multiple companies?
›
Yes. The platform supports multi-company setups with isolated data and team members per company.
